Gallery
=======

Here are examples of objects designed and fabricated with OpenVCAD.

Functionally Graded Lattice
---------------------------

Multi-material graded lattice structure made with soft, rigid, and non-curing
liquid materials.

Multi-material Stanford Bunny
-----------------------------

(a) OpenVCAD software render of the multi-material lattice-filled Stanford
Bunny; (b) physical artifact printed on a Stratasys J750 printer; and (c)
close-up view detailing graded internal lattice structures. Strut color is a
function of strut length. The bunny is comprised of 3,289 unique struts.

Inkjet 3D Printed Medical Scan
------------------------------

Real-world patient scan data processed with OpenVCAD and printed on an
Inkjet 3D Printer.

Functionally Graded Screwdriver
-------------------------------

A screwdriver with a continuous material gradient from rigid at the tip to
compliant at the grip.

Radial Gradient Gear
--------------------

A gear with a radial color gradient demonstrating spatial material control.

.. note::
   Visit the `OpenVCAD project page <https://matterassembly.org/openvcad>`__
   for high-resolution images of these artifacts.
